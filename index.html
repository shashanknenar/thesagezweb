OUR JOURNEY

CHENNAI WATER SHORTAGE JUNE,2019

...

As soon as the monsoon ended, Chennai’s water crisis was a global news. One of the biggest cities of India runs out of water. Water for drinking and irrigation is not sufficient now.
LACK OF SOLUTIONS

...

What solution does a water crisis have? Does it begin with finding who was at fault? Or by finding what actions made us land at such a situation? Nope, blame game is never a solution. A solution is what we would do further
OUR IDEA

...

Doing something such that the future generations would not have to suffer a similar crisis was the motive. Hence switching to a Sustainable Lifestyle with small steps every single day is what would make an impact.
OUR EXECUTION

With deforestation, soil loses the fertility and hence retention capacity. If we have taken so much from nature, it's time to give back. Thus seed products came into existence. With the use of each pencil, a tree is planted and hence we plant a life.
